Payment & Returns

Payment

TEMCo accepts the following payment methods: PayPal, Visa, MasterCard, AMEX, Discover.

We charge 9.75% sales tax for all orders shipping within California. Tax exemption is available if we are provided with a valid California resale certificate.

Returns

TEMCo allows for the return of products for 1 year from the date of purchase. Even if it's opened, broken, or used, we will still accept your return and pay shipping both ways.

We will replace the item with an identical item or issue a refund for the value of the item.
